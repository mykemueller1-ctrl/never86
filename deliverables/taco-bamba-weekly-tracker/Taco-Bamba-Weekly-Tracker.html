How to fill this out each week (~10 min)
  1. Sales (Toast): Pull the weekly sales summary. Enter Net sales, then split first-party (1P) vs third-party/delivery (3P), and the Toast Takeout / Thanx pickup line.
  2. Loyalty (Thanx dashboard): Copy the cumulative totals — members, customers in database, app downloads — and this week's reward redemptions.
  3. Lifecycle (Thanx): Enter the cumulative member buckets (onboarding / engaged / unengaged / bounced / churned). Note: these don't stabilize until ~90–120 days in.
  4. QR / channels: Enter the running (cumulative) scan totals per channel. The tool computes the weekly lift for you.
  5. Hit Save week. Everything else — trends, %s, charts, flags — updates automatically. Use Export to send a snapshot or back up your data.

Cumulative fields = the running total as shown in Thanx. The tool derives "new this week" from the change vs the prior week, so you never have to subtract by hand.
